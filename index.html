At ILB, we are dedicated to enabling cost-effective production of biopolymer from organic wastes. Although highly desirable and presenting a high market potential, production of biodegradable bioplastics is currently limited due to the high-cost of starch as feedstock relative to the low-cost of fossil-based feedstock. ILB’s technology makes it possible to overcome this barrier by replacing starch with organic wastes as the feedstock, thus significantly reducing the production cost while offering an alternative for more sustainable disposing of organic wastes.
